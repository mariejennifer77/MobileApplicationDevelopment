Planning on visiting Universal Studios in Orlando?

Be prepared to be overwhelmed. There are literally millions of places and activities to enjoy here. Also, the tickets rarely go on sale. If you think you can wait and find them at a lower price, you probably can't. We found the actual Universal site was the best deal we could find.
